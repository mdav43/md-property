Buying your first home is an exciting milestone, and the Sunshine Coast offers wonderful opportunities for first home buyers. Here's everything you need to know to make your first property purchase a success.

## Understanding Your Budget

Before you start house hunting, it's crucial to understand what you can afford. Consider:

- **Deposit requirements**: Aim for at least 10-20% of the purchase price
- **Stamp duty**: Factor in government fees and potential concessions for first home buyers
- **Ongoing costs**: Include insurance, rates, and maintenance in your budget
- **Finance pre-approval**: Get pre-approved to know your borrowing capacity

## First Home Buyer Benefits

Queensland offers several benefits for first home buyers:

- First Home Owner Grant for new homes
- Stamp duty concessions on eligible properties
- First Home Loan Deposit Scheme for qualifying buyers

## Choosing the Right Location

The Sunshine Coast offers diverse suburbs, each with unique characteristics:

- **Caloundra**: Family-friendly with beautiful beaches
- **Maroochydore**: Central hub with excellent amenities
- **Noosa**: Premium lifestyle location
- **Hinterland suburbs**: More affordable with peaceful settings

## The Buying Process

1. **Research**: Understand the market and identify target suburbs
2. **Finance**: Secure pre-approval from a lender
3. **Inspections**: Attend open homes and conduct building inspections
4. **Negotiation**: Work with your agent to secure the right price
5. **Settlement**: Complete legal requirements and take ownership

## Common First Home Buyer Mistakes to Avoid

- Overextending financially
- Skipping building and pest inspections
- Not researching the area thoroughly
- Making emotional rather than practical decisions
- Ignoring future resale potential
